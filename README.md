## Selected Projects

### 🔬 ILLARI — Corneal Topography & Keratoconus Detection
Integrated system for corneal surface analysis and early keratoconus diagnosis, optimized for deployment in rural and low-resource settings. Combines optical design, AI-based image analysis, and a scalable business model. Recognized at two independent competitions and presented at XPOSTEM-PUCP 2025.

`Python` `Computer Vision` `AI` `CAD/Fusion360` `Biodesign`

---

### 💓 CARDIOCALM — ECG Stress & Anxiety Monitoring
End-to-end pipeline for ECG signal acquisition, processing, and classification of stress-anxiety states using machine learning models. Developed as part of the Biomedical Signals course at UPCH.

`Python` `Signal Processing` `Machine Learning` `ECG`

---

### 📡 Ultrasound Speckle Reduction (Topics in Signal Processing)
Simulation and evaluation of speckle noise reduction methods in ultrasound imaging using synthetic MATLAB data and generative AI for image synthesis. Produced accompanying academic paper.

`MATLAB` `Python` `Medical Imaging` `Generative AI`

---

### 🧪 Colorimeter for Microfluidic Biochip Detection
Software tool developed during the UPCH research internship for automated colorimetric analysis of microfluidic chip responses, aimed at preeclampsia biomarker detection.

`Python` `Image Processing` `Microfluidics` `Lab Instrumentation`

---

### 🩺 CerviScan — POC Cervical Cancer Screening
Point-of-care diagnostic device proposal for cervical cancer screening in rural regions. First-place winner at Hackathon EMBS PASKA 2025; featured in RAS Insight UTEC magazine.

`Biodesign` `Hardware Prototyping` `Point-of-care Diagnostics`

---

### ⚗️ TEMAS-D — Green Hydrogen Generator Control
Cloud-based monitoring and control system for a green hydrogen generator, built on AWS microservices architecture. Developed as a semester-long systems project.

`TypeScript` `AWS` `Microservices` `IoT`

---

### 🤝 ISB 2025-II — Group 06 (Collaborator)
Introduction to Biomedical Signals course repository. Main contributor to signal processing implementations and documentation.

`Python` `MATLAB` `Biomedical Signals` → [View repository ↗](https://github.com/Lucero-Munive/GRUPO-06-ISB-2025-II)
